Privacy Policy (Mobile App)

This policy applies to REAL ESTATE MAROC mobile applications published by us.

Types of Data

Depending on app features, we may process: account information (name, email, phone), usage data (device, app interactions), and optional content you submit. Certain permissions (e.g., camera, files, location) are requested only when a feature requires them.

Purposes

To provide and improve app functionality, support customer service, ensure security and fraud prevention, and comply with legal obligations.

Permissions

If the app requests permissions (e.g., notifications, camera, storage, location), they are used only to enable related features. You can withdraw permissions via your device settings; some features may then be unavailable.

Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with processors (cloud hosting, analytics, crash reporting) under contractual safeguards, or as required by law.

Security & Retention

We apply technical and organizational measures to protect data and retain it only as long as necessary for the stated purposes or as required by law.

Your Choices & Rights

You can access, correct, or delete your data subject to applicable law. Contact us at support@reelestatemaroc.org for requests or questions.

International Transfers

When data is processed outside your jurisdiction, we implement appropriate safeguards consistent with applicable laws.

Updates

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our apps or legal requirements. The latest version will be available here with an effective date.
