Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-11-01

Overview

REAL ESTATE MAROC (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy. The app is designed to work offline for core features and does not send personal information to our servers.

The app works offline for viewing most content, but actions such as opening maps, sending emails, placing calls, or opening links may require an internet connection.

What the app does

  • Shows curated information about properties, services, and news/updates.
  • Provides on-device mortgage calculators.
  • Allows you to generate a company profile PDF and share news text using your device’s share options.
  • Offers Contact actions (Call, WhatsApp, Email, Map) that open your external apps.

Data collection

  • No analytics, advertising, tracking, or crash-reporting SDKs.
  • No server collection of personal data.
  • Calculator inputs and generated files/text are processed locally on your device.

Internet use & external apps

Most in-app content is available offline. Actions such as opening maps, sending an email, placing a call, or opening website links may require internet access and are handled by your chosen external app or website under its own privacy policy. We do not receive the content you send through those services.

Permissions

The app does not request dangerous runtime permissions. When you share a file, Android’s secure FileProvider grants temporary, read-only access to the specific file you choose.

Third-party services

  • In-App Review (Google Play) may be shown; we do not receive your personal information from this feature.
  • External apps you choose (e.g., dialer, WhatsApp, email, Maps) and websites operate under their own privacy policies.

Data sharing and transfers

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. No data leaves your device unless you choose to export or share it using your device’s share options.

Children’s privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect children’s personal information.

Changes

We may update this policy to reflect product or legal changes. The latest version and effective date will be shown in the app and on this page.
